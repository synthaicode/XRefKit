This page clarifies the boundary among four nearby documentation topics:

- group definitions
- flow-to-group mapping
- flow-internal quality assurance
- cross-flow structural feedback

  - defines what each group is allowed and responsible to do

- [System quality feedback loop](043_system_quality_feedback_loop.md#xid-8B31F02A4012)
