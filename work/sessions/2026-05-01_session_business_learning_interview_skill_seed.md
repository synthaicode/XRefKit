- purpose: create the first runnable repository Skill that lets AI learn business structure from a human through iterative questioning

## Observed Need

- business intake often begins before the human can explain the whole process
- requiring a complete process map blocks real use
- the missing reusable capability was a structured interview loop that converts
  fragments into business hypotheses and next questions

## Initial Trial Assumptions

- most first cycles can start from one bottleneck, one role, or one artifact
- the next best question is usually smaller than "explain the whole process"
- interview output should stay separate from later scoping output

## Open Gaps

- real use should confirm whether one question per cycle is always enough
- future promotion should observe whether the transition boundary to scoping is
  sharp enough in practice
